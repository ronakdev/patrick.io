Rationale behind Patrick_I/O

The problem is that Cochlear implants (CI's) are revolutionary tools for improving the quality of life for the profoundly deaf. However, calibrating and personalizing the auditory settings on CIs currently requires audiologists to rely solely on verbal and implicit communication from the patient.
